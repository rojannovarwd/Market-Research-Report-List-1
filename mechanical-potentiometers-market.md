<p><ul><li>Through Hole Potentiometers</li><li>SMD Potentiometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical potentiometers market is primarily divided into two types: through-hole potentiometers and surface mount device (SMD) potentiometers. Through-hole potentiometers feature leads that are inserted into drilled holes on a circuit board, ensuring robust connections for high-power applications. In contrast, SMD potentiometers are designed for mounting directly onto the surface of PCBs, optimizing space and enabling automated assembly. Both types cater to varying requirements in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications, driving growth in the potentiometer market.</p></p>
