<p><p>The sewage truck market consists of three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders, each designed for specific waste collection needs. Front loaders utilize a lifting mechanism at the front to scoop waste bins, ideal for high-density urban areas. Rear loaders feature a compaction system at the back, allowing for efficient loading and transportation of liquid waste. Side loaders have a lateral collection arm, making them suitable for automated operations and narrow street access. Each type offers distinct advantages in various operational contexts.</p></p>
