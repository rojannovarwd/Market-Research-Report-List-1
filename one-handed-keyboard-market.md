<p><strong>The One-handed Keyboard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Keyboard</li><li>Membrane Keyboard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The one-handed keyboard market primarily comprises two types: mechanical and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards utilize individual mechanical switches for each key, providing tactile feedback and durability, making them popular among gamers and typists who prefer precision. In contrast, membrane keyboards feature a pressure pad design, offering a quieter and more compact option, often at a lower cost. While mechanical keyboards excel in performance and longevity, membrane keyboards appeal to users seeking affordability and simplicity in a one-handed setup.</p></p>
