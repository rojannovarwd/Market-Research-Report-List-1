<p><strong>The Drum Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Leading Trailing Shoe Brake</li><li>Two Leading Shoe Brake</li><li>Servo Brake</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drum brakes are categorized into three main types based on their shoe configurations. Leading-trailing shoe brakes utilize one leading and one trailing shoe, providing balanced braking and lightweight design, ideal for passenger vehicles. Two leading shoe brakes have two leading shoes for enhanced braking performance, often used in heavier vehicles. Servo brakes incorporate a self-boosting mechanism, enhancing force application and efficiency, making them suitable for performance-oriented or high-demand applications, especially in commercial and heavy-duty vehicles.</p></p>
