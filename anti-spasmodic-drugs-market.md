<p><strong>The Anti-spasmodic Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antimuscarinics</li><li>Smooth Muscle Relaxants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-spasmodic drugs market comprises two main types: antimuscarinics and smooth muscle relaxants. Antimuscarinics work by blocking acetylcholine receptors in the gastrointestinal tract, reducing muscle contractions and alleviating symptoms of spasms. Smooth muscle relaxants, on the other hand, directly relax the smooth muscle tissue in the gastrointestinal and urinary tracts, providing relief from cramps and spasms. Both categories are crucial in managing conditions like irritable bowel syndrome and bladder disorders, offering significant therapeutic benefits.</p></p>
