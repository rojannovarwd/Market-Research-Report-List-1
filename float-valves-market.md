<p><p>Float valves are essential components in fluid control systems, available in two main types: angle float valves and round float valves. Angle float valves feature a 90-degree angle in their design, allowing for efficient installation in tight spaces, often used in tanks and cisterns. In contrast, round float valves have a cylindrical shape, providing versatility and ease of use in various applications, from irrigation to water supply systems. Both types ensure reliable regulation of fluid levels, enhancing system efficiency and performance.</p></p>
