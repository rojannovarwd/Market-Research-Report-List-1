<p><p>The rear view lens market encompasses two primary types of cameras: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and sensitivity to light, making them ideal for low-light conditions. Conversely, CMOS cameras are favored for their lower power consumption, smaller size, and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in modern applications. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing vehicle safety by providing clear rear views to prevent accidents during reversing.</p></p>
