<p><p>The blood glucose monitoring devices market comprises two main types: self-monitoring devices and continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) devices. Self-monitoring devices, such as glucose meters, allow individuals to manually check their blood sugar levels at specific times, providing immediate feedback. In contrast, continuous glucose monitoring devices measure glucose levels in real-time throughout the day and night, using a small sensor placed under the skin. This offers a comprehensive view of glucose trends, aiding in better diabetes management and personalized treatment adjustments.</p></p>
