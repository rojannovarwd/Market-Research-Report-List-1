<p><strong>The Automotive Crankshaft Position Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Position Sensor</li><li>Rotary Position Sensor</li><li>Proximity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive crankshaft position sensor market encompasses various types of sensors used to monitor the crankshaft's position and speed, essential for engine performance. Linear position sensors detect the crankshaft's position in a straight line, ideal for precise measurements. Rotary position sensors measure angular positions, providing critical data for ignition timing and fuel injection. Proximity sensors detect the presence of the crankshaft without direct contact, enhancing durability and reducing wear. Each type plays a pivotal role in optimizing engine efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
