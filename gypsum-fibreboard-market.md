<p><p>Gypsum Fibreboard is categorized into three main types: Regular, Moisture Resistant, and Fire Resistant. Regular Gypsum Fibreboard is primarily used for standard interior applications, providing a smooth surface for painting and finishing. Moisture Resistant Gypsum Fibreboard is designed for areas with higher humidity, such as bathrooms and kitchens, preventing warping and degradation. Fire Resistant Gypsum Fibreboard incorporates additives to enhance its fire-resistant properties, making it suitable for building codes in commercial and residential settings, ensuring safety and durability.</p></p>
