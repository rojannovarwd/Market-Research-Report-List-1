<p><p>PHA bioplastics are a group of biodegradable polymers derived from microbial fermentation. The primary types include PHB (polyhydroxybutyrate), known for its rigidity and thermal stability; PHBV (polyhydroxybutyrate-co-valerate), which offers increased flexibility; P34HB (a copolymer with enhanced properties); and PHBHHx (polyhydroxybutyrate-co-hexanoate), featuring improved elasticity. Each type has distinct characteristics suited for various applications, from packaging to textiles, contributing to a sustainable alternative to conventional plastics in the growing bioplastic market.</p></p>
