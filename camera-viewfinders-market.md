<p><strong>The Camera Viewfinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Viewfinders</li><li>Electronic Viewfinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera viewfinder market is primarily divided into two types: optical viewfinders (OVF) and electronic viewfinders (EVF). Optical viewfinders use a prism and mirrors to provide a direct optical sight of the scene, offering real-time feedback with no lag, making them popular in DSLR cameras. In contrast, electronic viewfinders display a digital preview of the image, including settings and effects, appealing to users of mirrorless systems for their versatility and ability to reflect changes in exposure settings instantly.</p></p>
