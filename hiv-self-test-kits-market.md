<p><p>The HIV self-test kits market includes various types such as lateral flow immunoassays, immunofiltration, and fluorescent chromatography. Lateral flow immunoassays are simple, portable tests that provide quick results through a visual readout. Immunofiltration techniques use a membrane to separate HIV antibodies from a sample for analysis. Fluorescent chromatography combines chromatography and fluorescence detection to enhance sensitivity and specificity. Each type caters to different user needs, providing accessible and reliable options for HIV self-testing.</p></p>
