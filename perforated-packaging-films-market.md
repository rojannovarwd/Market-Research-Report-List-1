<p><p>The perforated packaging films market includes various types based on the method of perforation. Laser perforation utilizes a focused laser beam to create precise holes, allowing for controlled airflow and moisture management. Needle perforation involves mechanically puncturing the film with needles, which can vary in size and pattern to achieve desired permeability. Other perforation methods may include mechanical or chemical techniques, catering to specific packaging needs in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and textiles, enhancing product preservation and shelf life.</p></p>
