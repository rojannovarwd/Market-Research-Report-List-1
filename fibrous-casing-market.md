<p><ul><li>Regular Fibrous Casing</li><li>Peelable Fibrous Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibrous casing market is primarily divided into two types: regular fibrous casing and peelable fibrous casing. Regular fibrous casing is used for traditional products, offering strength and support during processing and cooking, but remains in the final product. In contrast, peelable fibrous casing is designed to be easily removed after cooking, enhancing the presentation and texture of the final product. Both types are essential in the meat industry, catering to different product requirements and consumer preferences.</p></p>
