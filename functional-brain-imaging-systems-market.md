<p><p>The functional brain imaging systems market encompasses various technologies for observing brain activity. Functional MRI (fMRI) systems utilize magnetic resonance imaging to detect brain changes associated with blood flow. Functional Near Infrared Spectroscopy (fNIRS) employs light to measure changes in oxygenation and hemodynamics in the brain. Positron Emission Tomography (PET) systems use radioactive tracers to visualize metabolic processes. Other market types may include techniques like magnetoencephalography (MEG) and electroencephalography (EEG), offering complementary insights into brain function.</p></p>
