<p><strong>The Wire Terminals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Push-On Terminals</li><li>Butt Splice Connectors</li><li>Ring Terminals</li><li>Fork Terminals</li><li>Bullet Terminals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wire terminals market consists of various types designed for specific applications. Push-on terminals offer quick connections without tools, making them ideal for plug-and-play setups. Butt splice connectors join two wires end-to-end, ensuring a secure connection for electrical continuity. Ring terminals provide a secure attachment to a stud or screw, while fork terminals facilitate easy disconnection. Bullet terminals enable rapid connections and disconnections, making them useful in automotive and industrial settings. Each type serves distinct needs in electrical wiring applications.</p></p>
