<p><p>The phone batteries market primarily features two types: Lithium-Ion (Li-ion) and Nickel Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries. Li-ion batteries dominate due to their high energy density, lighter weight, and longer lifespan, making them ideal for modern smartphones. In contrast, NiMH batteries, while less common, are used in some older devices and specific applications due to their resistance to overheating and lower environmental impact. The growing demand for efficient and sustainable power sources is driving innovations in both battery types.</p></p>
