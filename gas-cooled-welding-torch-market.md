<p><p>The gas-cooled welding torch market includes three primary types: TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) torches, MIG (Metal Inert Gas) torches, and MAG (Metal Active Gas) torches. TIG torches provide precise control for welding thin materials, offering a clean finish without spatter. MIG torches are popular for rapid processes, using a continuous wire feed for thicker materials, while MAG torches employ active gas mixtures for better penetration and bonding in industrial applications. Each type serves distinct welding needs in various industries.</p></p>
