<p><ul><li>Mechanical Coalescers</li><li>Electrostatic Coalescers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coalescers are devices used to separate mixed liquid phases, primarily in oil and water separation processes. Mechanical coalescers utilize physical barriers and gravity to promote the merging of small droplets into larger ones, enhancing separation. In contrast, electrostatic coalescers employ electric fields to accelerate droplet merging, improving efficiency for challenging mixtures. Both market types are integral in various industries, including oil and gas, petrochemicals, and wastewater treatment, ensuring effective separation and purification of liquids to meet regulatory standards.</p></p>
