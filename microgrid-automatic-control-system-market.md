<p><p>The Microgrid Automatic Control System market primarily comprises two types: grid-connected and off-grid control systems. Grid-connected control systems enable the integration of microgrids with the main power grid, facilitating energy exchange and enhancing reliability. They typically support optimized energy management and demand response capabilities. In contrast, off-grid control systems operate independently, often in remote areas, providing localized energy solutions. These systems manage generation, storage, and consumption autonomously, ensuring energy supply without reliance on external grid infrastructure.</p></p>
