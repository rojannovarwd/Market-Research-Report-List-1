<p><strong>The Household Air Conditioner Fan Blades Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross Flow Fan Blades</li><li>Centrifugal Fan Blades</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household air conditioner fan blades market comprises primarily two types: cross flow fan blades and centrifugal fan blades. Cross flow fan blades are designed to move air across a wide area, providing even airflow distribution, typically used in wall-mounted AC units. In contrast, centrifugal fan blades draw air in through the center and expel it at high velocity, making them suitable for compact units requiring efficient cooling. Each type serves specific cooling needs, contributing to the overall efficiency of air conditioning systems.</p></p>
