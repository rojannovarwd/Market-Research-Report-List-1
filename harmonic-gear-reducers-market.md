Cup style reducers feature a compact design, ideal for applications requiring limited space. Hat style reducers, characterized by their toroidal shape, offer enhanced load capacity and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Pancake style reducers provide a slim profile and high torque output, perfect for robotics and aerospace applications. Each style serves distinct operational needs based on space, load, and application requirements.</p></p>
