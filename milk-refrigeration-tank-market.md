<p><p>The milk refrigeration tank market is primarily divided into closed type tanks and open type tanks. Closed type tanks are designed to maintain a controlled environment, preventing contamination and ensuring product integrity, making them ideal for large-scale dairy operations. In contrast, open type tanks offer easier access for cleaning and maintenance but may expose milk to external factors, increasing the risk of spoilage. Both types serve essential roles in the dairy supply chain, catering to different operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
