<p><strong>The HPLC Columns Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversed-Phase</li><li>Normal-Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HPLC columns market is primarily segmented into two types: reversed-phase and normal-phase columns. Reversed-phase columns utilize a non-polar stationary phase and a polar mobile phase, making them ideal for analyzing hydrophobic compounds. In contrast, normal-phase columns employ a polar stationary phase and a non-polar mobile phase, suitable for separating polar compounds. Each type serves distinct applications in pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and food safety, contributing to their specific market demands and growth trends within the analytical chemistry field.</p></p>
